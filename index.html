My Story

From market student to Guinness World Record holder

My journey with the stock market started the way most people's do — full of confusion, jargon, and expensive mistakes. Over the years, that hands-on experience turned into a mission: to empower every individual with financial knowledge, from basic stock market concepts to advanced investment strategies.

That mission led me to found Finowings, speak twice at TEDx, write India's best-selling finance book, and set a Guinness World Record along the way. Today, over 3 million people across platforms follow along as I break down real market knowledge in plain language.

Every video on this channel is built around one question — will this actually help someone make a better decision with their money?
